How to play Hand Cricket?

  • After the toss, if you win, you can choose between batting and balling.
  • There will be two players, you and the computer, if you choose balling, computer's will be batting and vice versa
  • Every match will have 2 innings. When you ball first, computer will bat first. In 2nd innings, this will be reversed
  • If you choose batting, when game starts you will have two seconds to score a run.
  • To score run, you press numbers from 1-6 which represents your run respectively.
  • Each time you score, runs will be kept on adding, if you don't press anything, its considered as a DOT ball (0 runs)
  • Simultaneously the computer too shows fingers representing number of runs.
  • When both you and computer score the same runs, the batsman is considered OUT.
  • When the batsman is OUT, he must ball next.
  • For ex: if you are out with 45 runs, the computer must score atleast 46 to win.
